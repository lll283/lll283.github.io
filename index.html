Authority & Power Matrix

  • Founders: Absolute authority. They have the final decision, Can override, terminate, or modify ANY decision.
  • Board of Directors: Can discipline, suspend, or demote ANY staff below Foundership.
  • Executives: Can restructure departments and enforce operational changes.
  • Management: Can issue strikes, warnings, activity notices.
  • Internal Affairs: Can investigate ANYONE except Founders.
  • Administrators: Can ban, kick, jail, warn.
  • Moderators: Can warn, mute, escalate.
  • Trainees: Observation only.

Chain of Command — Enforcement Rules

  • Chain of command must be respected at all times.
  • Do NOT bypass ranks unless an emergency exists.
  • Disputes go UP the chain — never sideways.
  • Refusal to follow lawful orders is insubordination.
  • Chain violations MUST be logged on Melonly.

Internal Affairs Division

Internal Affairs exists to protect the integrity of Los Angeles Roleplay. No rank is immune.

IA Investigation Flow

  • Complaint received
  • Evidence secured
  • Staff interviewed
  • Logs reviewed
  • Decision drafted
  • Board / Foundership approval

IA Authority

  • Access ALL logs
  • Freeze staff permissions
  • Override management during investigations
  • Recommend suspension, demotion, or termination

Management Division

  • Enforce staff activity requirements
  • Monitor professionalism and conduct
  • Issue strikes and warnings
  • Recommend promotions and demotions
  • Coordinate directly with IA

Management failure or negligence is treated as misconduct.

Disciplinary Escalation Logic

  • First offense → Warning
  • Repeated offense → Strike
  • Pattern behavior → Suspension
  • Abuse of power → Demotion
  • Severe abuse → Termination

Severity overrides progression.

Staff Expectations

  • Professional conduct at all times
  • No ego or power abuse
  • Neutral and evidence-based enforcement
  • Strict chain of command compliance
  • Melonly logging is mandatory
  • Confidentiality is absolute
