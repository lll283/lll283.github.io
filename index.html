      Rules

      • Discord Rules (Roleplay & Server Conduct)
        • 1. Only roleplay in designated channels.
        • 2. Stay in-character; no breaking immersion unnecessarily.
        • 3. No powergaming or metagaming.
          Powergaming: Forcing actions without consent.
          Metagaming: Using OOC info IC.
        • 4. Respect staff hierarchy.
        • 5. No spamming, NSFW content, or excessive pings.
        • 6. Follow voice chat guidelines and push-to-talk if recommended.
        • 7. Follow staff instructions, appeal privately.
        • 8. Do not share personal info, doxx, or threaten others.
        • 9. Participate in events properly, no disruption.
        • 10. No cheating, exploiting, or glitches.
        • 11. Rule violations may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans.
        • 12. Questions? Ask staff in #questions channel.
