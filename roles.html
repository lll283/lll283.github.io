Chain of Command

All staff must follow the established chain of command. Skipping ranks without valid reason may result in discipline.

Authority Flow

  • Founder / Co-Founder
  • Assistant Founder / Assistant Co-Founder
  • Chairman of the Board
  • Director → Deputy Director → Assistant Director
  • Executive Director → Deputy Executive Director
  • Chief of Staff / Executive Officer
  • Management Team
  • Internal Affairs / Administration
  • Moderation Team
  • Staff Trainees

Escalation Rules

  • Issues must be handled at the lowest possible level first.
  • Only escalate if unresolved or if severity requires it.
  • Founders may intervene at any time.
  • Internal Affairs operates independently when assigned.

Roles & Responsibilities

Each team has defined responsibilities. Acting outside your authority is prohibited.

Staff Trainees

@Staff Trainee

Learns procedures, observes staff actions, and operates under supervision. Trainees have no independent enforcement authority.

Moderation Team

Head / Senior / Junior / Trial Moderator

Handles minor rule violations, chat moderation, basic in-game enforcement, and reports escalated issues to Administration.

Scope

Moderators do not issue severe punishments unless explicitly authorized. Abuse of moderation powers results in disciplinary action.

Administrative Team

Head / Senior / Junior / Trial Administrator

Enforces server rules, issues punishments, documents actions via Melonly, and ensures fair and consistent moderation.

Limitations

Administrators may not overrule Management or Internal Affairs unless authorized. All actions must be logged.

Internal Affairs Team

Director / Senior / Junior / Trial Internal Affairs

Handles staff investigations, misconduct cases, corruption reports, abuse of power, confidentiality breaches, and appeals reviews. Internal Affairs operates independently from Management.

Authority

Internal Affairs may investigate any rank below Foundership unless restricted by Founders or the Board. All staff are required to cooperate fully.

Management Team

Head / Senior / Junior / Trial Management

Oversees day-to-day staff operations, activity tracking, performance reviews, and department coordination. Management handles routine staff discipline, guidance, and internal communication.

Community Manager & Staff Supervisor

Ensures staff professionalism, community engagement, and adherence to standards. Acts as liaison between staff and executive leadership.
