Roleplay Rules

1. RDM – Random Deathmatch
Rule: Killing another player without IC reason or proper RP context.
Punishments:
1st offense: Warning + temporary IC probation
2nd offense: Kick from server
3rd offense / severe: 7-day ban

2. VDM – Vehicle Deathmatch
Rule: Using a vehicle to kill/injure players outside RP.
Punishments:
1st: Warning + RP apology
2nd: Temporary RP ban (1–3 days)
3rd / severe: 3–7 day server ban

3. FRP – Fail Roleplay
Rule: Acting unrealistic or breaking RP protocols.
Punishments:
Minor: Warning + reminder
Moderate: 12–24 hour RP mute
Severe: 3-day RP ban

4. MG – Metagaming
Rule: Using OOC knowledge in-character.
Punishments:
1st: Warning
2nd: RP mute (1–12h)
3rd / severe: 1–3 day RP ban

5. PG – Powergaming
Rule: Forcing actions on others without consent.
Punishments:
1st: Warning + IC apology
2nd: 12–24h RP mute
3rd: 1–3 day RP ban

6. GTAD – IC Theft / Criminal Actions
Rule: Vehicle theft, robbery, or other crimes must have RP justification.
Punishments:
Minor: Warning + IC fine/arrest
Moderate: 1–2 day RP ban
Severe: 3–7 day RP ban

7. CPT – Combat / Weapon Misuse
Rule: Weapons must be used realistically.
Punishments:
Minor: Warning + IC apology
Moderate: 1-day RP ban
Severe: 3-day RP ban + IC penalties

8. Emergency Services Conduct
Rule: EMS, Police, Dispatch must follow RP procedures.
Punishments:
Minor: Warning
Moderate: Temporary RP suspension (1–12h)
Severe: 1–3 day RP ban

Discord / OOC Rules

1. Respect & Conduct
Rule: No harassment, bullying, hate speech, discrimination.
Punishments:
Minor: Warning
Moderate: 1-hour mute
Severe: 24-hour mute → Kick

2. Spam / Advertising
Rule: No spam, mass pings, or advertising.
Punishments:
Minor: Warning
Moderate: 1-hour mute / content deletion
Severe: Kick / temporary ban

3. NSFW / Inappropriate Content
Rule: No NSFW, gore, or illegal content.
Punishments:
1st offense: 1 month ban
2nd offense: Permanent ban

4. Staff Impersonation / Disobedience
Rule: Do not impersonate staff or ignore instructions.
Punishments:
1st: Warning
Moderate: Kick
Severe: Permanent ban

5. Doxxing / Personal Info Sharing
Rule: Never share personal info.
Punishments:
1st: Kick
Severe: Permanent ban

6. Channel Usage
Rule: Keep messages in correct channels.
Punishments:
Minor: Warning
Moderate: 30-min – 1-hour mute
Severe: Kick

7. Voice Chat Rules
Rule: Avoid trolling, harassment, or disruptive noises.
Punishments:
Minor: Warning
Moderate: VC mute / temp server mute
Severe: Kick / VC Ban
