LARP Staff Governance Manual

This document is the sole authoritative governance manual for all staff members of Los Angeles Roleplay (LARP). It applies to every staff rank without exception.

This system is designed for a large-scale operation exceeding 800 staff members and exists to enforce consistency, accountability, realism, and professionalism.

Claiming ignorance, misunderstanding, or personal interpretation is not a defense.

Authority & Power

  • Owners hold absolute authority. Their decisions are final.
  • No staff member may override a higher rank.
  • Emergency actions may be taken but must be logged.
  • Abuse of authority results in immediate removal.

Staff Structure

  • Owner
  • Executive Administration
  • Senior Administration
  • Administration
  • Moderator
  • Trial Moderator

Rank does not grant immunity.

Ethics & Integrity

  • No favoritism, bias, or immunity
  • No abuse of commands
  • No retaliation against players or staff
  • No public staff arguments
  • No leaking internal information
  • No bribery, corruption, or special treatment

Operational Standards

  • Investigate before enforcing
  • Use the least disruptive solution first
  • Respect ongoing roleplay scenes
  • Remain calm, professional, and neutral
  • Escalate uncertainty immediately

ER:LC Roleplay Rules

  • No Fail Roleplay
  • No Random Deathmatch (RDM)
  • No Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)
  • No Metagaming or Powergaming
  • No Unrealistic Driving
  • No Exploiting or Glitch Abuse

Moderation Doctrine

  • Evidence-based enforcement only
  • Warnings when appropriate
  • Immediate action for severe violations
  • Consistency across all staff
  • Documentation required for major actions

Punishment Matrix

  • Minor → Warning / Jail
  • Moderate → Kick / Temporary Ban
  • Severe → Long-Term or Permanent Ban
  • Exploiting → Immediate Permanent Ban

Context, intent, and history must always be considered.

Melonly Logging System

Melonly is the official and mandatory logging platform for Los Angeles Roleplay. All major staff actions must be logged accurately and truthfully.

  • All bans must be logged on Melonly
  • Jails exceeding standard duration must be logged
  • Warnings contributing to escalation must be logged
  • Evidence (screenshots or clips) is required
  • Falsifying or omitting logs is grounds for termination

If it is not logged on Melonly, it did not happen.

Appeals & Reviews

  • Appeals must be respectful and evidence-based
  • Appeals are reviewed by senior staff or owners
  • False or malicious appeals may worsen punishment
  • Owners retain final appeal authority

Activity & Availability

  • Minimum weekly activity required
  • 7 days inactivity → Notice
  • 14 days inactivity → Demotion review
  • 30 days inactivity → Removal

Internal Discipline

  • Verbal Warning
  • Written Warning
  • Strike
  • Suspension
  • Demotion
  • Termination

Termination & Exit

  • Immediate permission removal
  • No retaliation or sabotage
  • No leaking internal data
  • Violations may be reported to platform authorities

Security & Confidentiality

  • Staff systems are confidential
  • No sharing credentials or panels
  • No screenshots of staff chats
  • Security violations result in termination

Final Authority

Ownership reserves the right to modify this document at any time. Continued service implies acceptance of all revisions.
